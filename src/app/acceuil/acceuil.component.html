About Us

ENSIT Geeks Club was founded in 2021 by Nizar Essid at the National High School of Tunis. It brings together people interested in sharing their knowledge and helping each other in IT. It welcomes both beginners and experts. It aims to deepen your knowledge necessary in IT to be able to succeed in the professional field and allow students to use today's technology to prepare for the future

Activities

Mobile Dev

As in 2021, mobile phones are the most used devices in order to connect to the Internet through mobile applications. Because we are aware of the spread of mobile apps, we help our members to learn how to develop applications using frameworks and SDKs such as flutter.

Application Dev

We know that programming is becoming more and more important in our world and we know that everyone needs it, that's why we organize training sessions and weekly workshops to improve your coding skills, web development , app building and more.

Game Dev

Games making becomes an important goal for geeks. That's why, Ensit Geeks Club has carried out trainings, workshops to master the game development using the unity engine, which is very popular, easy to use and practical for beginners and experts.
